Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberry, and Brie

wow your taste buds whith this festive focaccia appetiezer topped with sweet Caramelized onions, tart
Cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for pan

For the toppings

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrap rind off with a pairing knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
